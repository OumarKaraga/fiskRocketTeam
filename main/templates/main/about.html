About Us

NASA Student Lauch Initiative Competition

Flight Readiness Review(FRR)

The Fisk Altitude Achievement Missile Team (FAAMT aka Rocket Team) is a rocket program at Fisk University created to compete in the NASA sponsored University Launch Initiative (USLI). The competition includes design, construction, and launch of a rocket to take a scientific payload to an altitude of exactly one mile (5,280 feet). Competing schools engage in educational activities that promote an interest in science, engineering, and spaceflight in younger students.

This student engagement, a series of educational outreach events planned on a biweekly basis, has reached thousands of students in the Nashville Metropolitan area. It continues to bring science to hundreds of students each year in the form of hands on activities, presentations, and interactions between college age students and younger students.

Rocket Team also enables collaboration between STEM and liberal arts students within and beyond Fisk University. Students of various academic persuasions participate in Rocket Team, from Engineering and Biology to English and Art.
